风湿病学家负责研究、诊断和治疗人体关节、肌肉和软组织的疾病、损伤和缺陷。需要注册或许可证。

> Investigates, diagnoses and treats diseases, injuries and deficiencies of human joints, muscles and soft tissue. Registration or licensing is required.
