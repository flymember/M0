### ANZSCO 254413 注册护士（儿童和家庭健康） Registered Nurse (Child and Family Health) - FLYabroad ###

####  [254413 注册护士（儿童和家庭健康）职业描述 Job description - FLYabroad](http://www.flyabroadvisa.com/anzsco/2544.html#254413)

注册护士（儿童和家庭健康）负责向儿童提供从出生到上学期间的护理以及以家庭为重点的预防，早发现，早治疗。为受到身体、心理和社会问题影响的儿童和他们的家人提供援助。需要注册或许可证。

> Provides nursing care to children from birth to school age, and their families, with an emphasis on the prevention, early detection of, and early intervention in physical, emotional and social problems affecting children and their families such as assistance with parentcraft, immunisation and developmental milestones. Nursing or Midwifery registration or licensing is required.

Previously referred to in ASCO as:
2323-11 Registered Nurse
